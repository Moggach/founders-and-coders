About Me

I am currently a metadata manager working in book publishing and I am looking to change careers to become a software developer. For the last two years I have been teaching myself the basics of HTML, CSS and Javascript. Learning to code has changed my life - boosting my confidence and opening up so many opportunities. I would like to join the collaborative Founders and Coders environment to push my skills further.

What can I offer the course?

I have demonstrated an interest in software development as a career and an aptitude for self-directed learning. Last year I built a personal blog site with Gatsby and I am currently working on a React application. This app uses the Google Books API to return book data based on the user’s search input. Please see the Projects page for details. Next I would like to finish the Javascript Algorithms and Data Structures Certification on FreeCodeCamp.

What would I like to achieve?

I am particularly interested in your Tech for Better programme. Alongside my full time job in publishing I also work one day a week on the National Domestic Abuse Helpline. I would like to use tech solutions to improve the safety of women and children experiencing domestic abuse. I would like to develop an app that could be discreetly installed on a survivor’s phone and could enable her to create a personalised safety plan; connect her to specialist services and more.

Coding + Publishing

I have worked hard to raise the profile of coding and tech literacy within book publishing. Each year I participate in the Consonance app initiative Side Project Summer and in May 2020 I gave an interview to the publishing community BookMachine on my journey into coding so far. I was a volunteer coach at the FutureBook Day of Code in November 2019 where 40 delegates from across publishing built a website showcasing their own book data.

Thank you for reading my application and I hope to hear from you soon.
